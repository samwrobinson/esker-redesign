Vermont Website Design

Esker Designs - Vermont Website Design

Hand-Coded Websites That Work For Vermont Businesses

I'm Sam Robinson, a Vermont web developer who believes in doing things the right way. My journey into web development started unexpectedly during a Vermont winter when my wife tore her ACL. While she recovered in our cozy 350-square-foot apartment, I discovered my passion for coding, waking up at 5 AM every day to learn the craft of building websites properly.

What struck me was how many Vermont businesses were stuck choosing between expensive web agencies or frustrating DIY website builders. I saw local business owners struggling with Wix and Squarespace, trying to manage websites that just weren't performing. That's when I realized: Vermont businesses deserve better.

Through my learning, I discovered that clean, hand-coded websites consistently outperform clunky page builders. They're faster, more secure, and actually help businesses grow. That's why I decided to start Esker Designs - a Vermont website design company built specifically for local businesses.
